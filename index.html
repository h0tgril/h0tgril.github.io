Featured maps:
kk_lemonade: a vapor dream
Inspired by the Miami Pink House, this unique little map will keep you on your toes with mostly close-quarters combat!
kk_gamertime: the 1337 gamer condo
The fan-favorite Deathmatch (DM) game mode map! This messy condo is perfect for hectic free-for-alls!
hg_green: an abandoned mansion
This is hot_gril's signature map, with a front yard, large atrium, balcony, bedrooms, and basement offering mid to long range combat and plenty of grates to shoot through!
kk_davy_jones: a cursed pirate ship
Arrr! This here cursed map be havin' three treacherous levels and a foul bilge brimming with surprises, matey! Take yer shots from afar, or plunge below deck to take matters into yer own hands!
kk_souk: an Arabian bazaar
Inspired by Kim's recent trip to Arabia, this souk offers both narrow alleys and long shootout zones!
kk_italy: a castle town
This map is basically a long-range shootout between the blue team in the castle and the red team in the streets! Climb the flags up to the walls, or charge through the gate!
hg_minecraft: a Minecraft town
The town walls have been breached! Battle it out through a large number of avenues, hide inside buildings, and use loose blocks for cover!
kk_haunted_mansion: a spooky mansion
Based on Luigi's Mansion, this supernatural map features two main hallways and several rooms with plenty of tricks up their sleeves! Drop through the carpet, hide in the indoor-outdoor room, climb through the sewer, or sneak past enemies in the front yard. Look out for flying chairs and possessed pianos!
kk_complex: Pripyat grad student housing
Inspired by Kim's travels to ex-Soviet countries and her previous depressing apartment, this abandoned housing complex combines creepy brutalist architecture and home furnishings. Climb the elevator shaft, jump atop the concrete statue, or hide in the bathroom!
